About Speak EU

Speak EU is a comprehensive language learning platform designed specifically for Bangladeshi immigrants and workers who want to learn European languages.

Features:

  • Bengali pronunciation guides for all vocabulary
  • 45+ European languages
  • Favorites system to save important words
  • Export/Import functionality
  • Dark/Light theme support
  • Mobile-friendly design

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Speak EU respects your privacy. We only store data locally on your device:

  • Favorite vocabulary items
  • Theme preferences
  • Language learning progress

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to enhance your learning experience and is never shared with third parties.

Data Control

You have full control over your data and can reset it anytime using the "Reset All Data" option in the menu.
